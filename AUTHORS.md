# Authors & Credits

## Original game

Olympia was created by **Rich Skrenta** (Copyright 1993 — the original sources
are public domain). The G2 engine in this repository is the second-generation
Olympia play-by-mail strategy game engine, the successor to G1 and the ancestor
of the later G3 and TAG engines.
